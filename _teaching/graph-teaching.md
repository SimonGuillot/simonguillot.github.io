Graph Theory
======
18 hours of Tutorials for 3rd-year CompSci students : the first part of the course is dedicated to the exploration of basic graph theory concepts (connexity, shortest paths, BFS/DFS centrality, clustering and community detection), while the second part of the course focuses on applied usecases of those concepts in text representation and epidemiology.
