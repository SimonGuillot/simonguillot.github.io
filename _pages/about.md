About me
======
I'm a researcher working on computational semantics and word representations interpretability issues. In particular, I am interested in extending interpretability research on vector representations to the vectors themselves, and in how such vectors can be meaningfully interpreted by non-expert human users. Conversely, I wish to use interpretable word vectors to derive new observables for text-as-data fields, notably textual semantics (diachronic and synchronic) and cognitive science.

I am currently finishing my PhD at [LIUM](https://lium.univ-lemans.fr/lium/lst/) in the Language and Speech Technologies team. I'm working there with Nicolas Dugué (LIUM), Antoine Laurent (LIUM/Pyannote) and Mathieu Valette (INALCO) to develop the usage of interpretable representations in humanities, and cognitively informed linguistics research.
